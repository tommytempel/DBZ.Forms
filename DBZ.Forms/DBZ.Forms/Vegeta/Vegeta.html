Vegeta and His Transformations

Vegeta Base Form

This is Vegeta in his Base form when he fist arrived on Earth. He was a very powerful foe that even went as far as killing Nappa when Goku paralyzed him.

Vegeta's Great Ape Form

This is Vegeta in his Great Ape Form. He realized Goku was a much bigget threat than he realized.

Vegeta's Super Saiyan Form

This is was introdueced when Vegeta arrived to fight the Androids. It was a powerful moment for him as his life long goal was to become a super saiyan.

Super Vegeta

Vegeta's ascended form. His ego was also Ascended.

Vegeta Super Saiyan 2

This was when Vegeta allowed himself to be controlled by Babadis Majic. Vegeta's goal was to fight Goku before he returned to Other World.

Vegeta's Super Saiyan God Form

Vegeta's God Form. He obtained this off camera. This form is rarely used as he tends to favor Super Saiyan Blue or Ultra Ego.

Super Saiyan Blue

This is Vegeta's Super Saiyan Blue form that we first seen him transform into in his fight against Frieza. He learned this form training with Goku.

Super Saiyan Blue Evolved

Vegeta Ascended Super Saiyan Blue form that he used against Toppo.

Ultra Ego

Vegeta's Ultra Ego form that he debuted recently. Not much has been seen of this form and it has yet to make it to the anime. This form utilizes the Power of Destruction.
