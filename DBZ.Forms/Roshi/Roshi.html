Master Roshi and His Power

Master Roshi

Master Roshi, the teacher of Gohan(Goku's Grandpa), The Ox King, Goku, Krillin and Yamcha. In his day he was regarded as the most powerful of his time. He was not to be taken lightly.

Master Roshi Full Power

This is Roshi when he is using his full power. He used this to put out the fire on the Ox King's Mountain and to destroy the moon when Goku transfomed into an ape. This form is also shown in Dragon Ball Super which is where the above photos comes from.
