Majin Buu and His Different Forms

Majin Buu

This is the first form we see Majin Buu in. You can blame Goku and Vegeta for him appearing. But he's a good guy now so please thank them.

Fit Buu

This is Majn Buu when he trains. Unfortently, he fell asleep before the Tournament of Power so we did not get a chance to see it in action.

Evil Buu

This is Buu's Evil Energy that was released when the dog was shot. (The dog was healed.)

Super Buu

This was the form Evil Buu took after absorbing Majin Buu.

Kid Buu

This is the original form of Majin Buu. He was later reincarnated as a Human named Uub per Goku's request.
